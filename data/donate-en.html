OBS Zoom Filter Plugin

Simple and convenient camera zoom control for your OBS streams and recordings

Convenient Zoom Control

Quickly zoom in and out of your scene using hotkeys with smooth transitions for a professional streaming experience.

Customizable Zoom Steps

Customize single-click and continuous zoom speeds (up to 5.0x) according to your needs for precise zoom control.

Smooth Transition Animation

Three smooth transition modes (linear, exponential, logarithmic) with start speed, deceleration and overshoot controls to create professional camera effects.

Mouse Tracking Smoothness

Configure mouse tracking smoothness to reduce jitter and provide stable, fluid camera movements when following mouse position.

Auto Reset Function

Automatically revert to the original view size after a specified time, no manual operation needed, making your stream more comfortable.

Advanced Animation Effects

Support for elastic overshoot effects that create a subtle bounce feeling during zoom transitions, adding dynamic visual interest to your streams.
